About Us

Welcome to our family run business

We pride ourselves on providing a personal service from your initial enquiry through to the finished product. At Ginghams we create beautifully handmade curtains which can be lined, inter-lined and with a choice of different headings. Loose covers, Roman blinds and cushions are all tailor made and finished to your individual requirements. We offer a vast selection of fabrics ranging from Romo, Villa Nova, Illiv, Harlequin, Sanderson, The Pure Edit and Prestigious Textiles to name but a few! Or if you prefer we are happy to make up in your own fabric.

Complete your soft furnishings with our selection of poles, wallpapers, throws and trimming accessories.

Our aim is to deliver the high expectation of our customers and is renewed for our attention to detail.

You are welcome to visit us at our Studio, Unit 5, The Old Potato Yard, Planks Farm, alternatively samples can be brought to you so that you can browse in the comfort of your own home. Ginghams offer services in and around Devizes, Marlborough, Melksham, Chippenham, Calne and surrounding villages.
